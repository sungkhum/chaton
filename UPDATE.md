ChatOn Update — What's New

Instant loading — Conversations now appear immediately when you open the app. No more staring at a spinner while messages decrypt. Previews load progressively in the background.

Unread message divider — When you open a chat with unread messages, a clear divider shows you exactly where you left off.

Mention and reaction indicators — Conversations where someone @mentioned you or reacted to one of your messages now show indicators in the sidebar so you can spot what needs your attention. Inside the chat, floating buttons let you jump directly to each unread mention or reaction, cycling through them one by one with a highlight flash — Telegram-style.

Rich tweet previews — Share an X/Twitter link and it renders inline with the author, tweet text, and engagement stats. No more mystery links.

Tipping now supports ChatOn — Tips of $0.10+ include a 10% platform fee to fund development, bundled atomically on-chain in a single transaction. Fully transparent — the split is shown before you confirm.

Better group joining — After requesting to join a group via invite link, you're redirected into the app with a confirmation modal instead of being stuck on a separate page.

Voice messages — Tap the mic button to record and send audio clips, Telegram-style. Recordings are compressed to tiny 64kbps AAC files for fast uploads. Playback shows a waveform with seek-by-tap and duration. Compatible with Focus and other DeSo messaging apps.

Push notification fix — Eliminated duplicate notifications caused by browsers rotating push subscription endpoints.
