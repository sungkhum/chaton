ChatOn Update — What's New

Group Chats

• Pin messages — Group owners can pin an important message to the top of the chat. Everyone in the group sees it front and center. Tap to jump straight to it.

• Quick DM from groups — See something in a group chat you want to follow up on privately? Tap any message and hit "Message @username" to start a one-on-one conversation.

• Sorted member list — Group members now appear in order of who's been most active, so you always know who's around.

Messages

• Translate any message — Tap "Translate" in the message menu to convert any message to your language. One tap, instant result.

• Auto-translate — Turn on auto-translate in settings and foreign messages translate themselves as they arrive. No tapping required.

• Tap to jump to replies — When someone quotes a message, tap the quote to scroll straight to the original. A quick highlight shows you exactly where you landed.

• Delivery status — Watch your messages go from sending to sent to confirmed, right below each bubble. No more wondering if it went through.

• Smarter image sizing — Photos now display at their natural size instead of being crammed into one fixed width. Small screenshots stay small. Big photos go big.

Reactions

• See who reacted — Tap any reaction to see exactly who left it. On mobile it slides up as a sheet; on desktop you get a detail card.

Navigation

• Swipe to go back — On mobile, swipe from the left edge to slide back to your chat list. Feels like a native app.

• Smoother transitions — Opening and closing chats on mobile now uses a polished slide animation instead of the old hard cut.

Links

• Reddit previews — Drop a Reddit link and ChatOn pulls in the title, content, and subreddit automatically. No more mystery links.

• Join link cards — Share a ChatOn group invite in a message and it shows up as a rich card with the group name and a join button, right in the chat.

Profile

• Edit your profile — Change your display name, profile picture, and bio without leaving the app. Also part of the new onboarding setup for first-time users.

Reliability

• Offline mode — Lose your connection? ChatOn now shows a proper offline screen instead of breaking. Pick up right where you left off when you're back online.

• Better notifications — Push notifications are more reliable across devices, especially on iPhones and iPads.
