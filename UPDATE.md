ChatOn Update — What's New

• Message translation — Messages in other languages can now be translated with one tap. Hit "Translate" in the message menu to convert any message to your language.

• Auto-translate — Turn on auto-translate in settings and foreign messages translate themselves as they arrive. No tapping required.

• Language preference — Set your preferred language in settings (globe icon). ChatOn defaults to your device language.

• Smart detection — Every message you send is tagged with its language automatically, so other users can translate it instantly without delay.
